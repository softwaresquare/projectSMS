Code of Conduct for the Students

             Caspian Valley College expects all the students to comply with the following codes of conduct. Breach of these codes of conduct will lead a student to the conditions as mentioned with the code of conduct or the decision to be taken by the college. Their signature indicates their confirmation to compulsory adherence to these codes of conducts.

Attendance & Punctuality

1. The HSEB attendance policy requires the student to have a minimum of 80% attendance of the classes actually held. So students are required to meet the criteria.
2. Students having less than mentioned percentage of attendance will not be eligible to sit for the final examinations.
3. Students who has been detained due to shortage of attendance shall not be allowed to be promoted to the next year and he/she will be required to take readmission and repeat all courses of the said year with the next batch of students.
4. The students are expected to be punctual. They are required to be within the college premises five minutes before the starting time.
5. Students are not allowed to leave their classes in the middle of class hours. Once the students enter the college, will not be allowed to leave it until the ending time except for the exception cases.
6. During the leisure periods, students are expected to remain inside the class doing something related to their study. They are not allowed to hang around canteen or louse in the premises without permission.

Uniform

• All the students are required to be in the proper uniform prescribed by the college.
• No students is allowed to take the class or remain in the college premises without proper
uniform if not mentioned otherwise.
Assignments & Examinations
• The assignments are consisted of two types: Theoretical and Practical. All the students are required to complete all the assigned works within the deadline given.
• The students should compulsorily attend all the internal examinations. Any students who misses such exams will be suspended from the college unless the re-examinations are attended by paying the fine.

Declaration by Parents/Guardian and the Student
I understand that my child's enrollment into the college will bind him to all the rules and regulations of the college and I will try at my best to pursued him to pursue them. As the Guardian/Parents of the student, I will not interfere in the justifiable actions taken by the college for the benefit of both the students and the institution.
